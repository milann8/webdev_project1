Why does Milan love music?

Music is honestly a huge part of my life because it can change my whole mood in like two seconds. Whether I’m sad, mad, or happy, there’s always a song that just gets it. That’s why I love it so much, it’s like my personal escape from everything.

My Favorite Songs + why

  1. "Close to you" by Dreezy ft. T.Pain
    • This is my all-time favorite song. I gatekeep this song because I love it so much. It is the perfect late night song!
  2. "Sweet Thing" by Mary J. Blige
    • This song always lifts up my spirits. It is a "happy" song! This is the song I go to when I am feeling down because as soon as I hear the first three notes of the guitar, it feels as if nothing else matters.
  3. "Love" by Keyshia Cole
    • It’s one of those songs you blast when you're in your feelings, and it never gets old. I've been singing this song with my heart since a kid!
